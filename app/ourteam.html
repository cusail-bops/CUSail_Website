Who We Are

We are a team of 34 undergraduate students at Cornell University who share a passion to build the next generation of robotic, intelligent sailboats.

The team leads are responsible for managing their respective subteams. They plan out and assign tasks to members and ensure the team is fully prepared for the competition each June and is making progress on the long term goals.
